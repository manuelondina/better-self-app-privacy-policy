Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 22, 2026

Introduction

Better Self App ("we", "our", or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and how it is shared.

Information We Collect

The App collects daily activity information such as workout data, diet logs, and other actions you record. Some actions may involve capturing photos via your device camera.

We do not collect or store any personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or location.

Apple Health and HealthKit Integration

Better Self App integrates with Apple Health (HealthKit) to provide automatic health data tracking. This section explains exactly how we use your health data.

Data We Access from Apple Health

With your explicit permission, the App reads the following data types from Apple HealthKit:

  • Sleep Analysis: Sleep duration, sleep stages, and sleep quality metrics to calculate your daily sleep points
  • Workout Data: Workout type, duration, and energy burned to calculate your daily workout points

How We Use Your Health Data

  • Calculate activity points based on your sleep duration and workout intensity
  • Display your health metrics within the App with clear "from Apple Health" attribution
  • Track your daily progress and streaks
  • Include aggregated point values (not raw health data) in leaderboard rankings

How We Protect Your Health Data

  • Local Processing: All health data is processed locally on your device
  • No Raw Data Storage: We do NOT store raw health data (sleep times, workout details) on our servers
  • Points Only: Only aggregated point values derived from health data are stored for leaderboard purposes
  • No Third-Party Sharing: Your health data is never shared with third parties, advertisers, or data brokers
  • No Marketing Use: Health data is never used for marketing or advertising purposes

Your Control Over Health Data

You have full control over your Apple Health data:

  • Opt-in Only: Apple Health integration requires your explicit permission
  • Revoke Anytime: You can disconnect Apple Health at any time in the App settings or through your device's Settings > Privacy > Health
  • Selective Permissions: You can grant or deny access to specific data types (sleep, workouts) independently

For more information about how Apple protects your health data, visit Apple's Health Privacy page.

We do not collect or store any personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or location.

How We Use Your Data

  • Track your daily progress for self-improvement.
  • Calculate points based on your recorded actions and health data.
  • Display points on a leaderboard shared with other users.
  • Unlock achievements based on your activity milestones.

Your data is anonymized and only used in aggregate to maintain the leaderboard and personal progress tracking.

Data Sharing

We do not share any personal information with third parties. The only shared data is the anonymized activity points for the leaderboard.

Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your activity data, but no system can guarantee complete security.

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
