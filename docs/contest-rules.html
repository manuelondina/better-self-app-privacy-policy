Official Contest Rules

Last Updated: January 6, 2026

1. Eligibility

Contests are open to individuals aged 18 or older. Contests are available worldwide where permitted by law.

2. Contest Period

Each contest runs on a weekly basis. The start and end times are displayed within the App.

3. Entry and Participation

Participation in prize contests requires a paid entry via Apple In-App Purchase. Entry fees are non-refundable.

Payment grants eligibility to compete but does not guarantee winning a prize.

4. How Points Are Earned

Points are earned by completing and logging daily activities as defined in the App. Photo submissions may be required to verify completion.

5. Verification and Fair Play

Submitted activities and photos may be reviewed to ensure fairness and accuracy. Fraudulent or misleading submissions may result in disqualification.

6. Winner Determination

Winners are ranked based on total points earned during the contest period. Rankings are final.

In the event of a tie, placement is determined by consistency of participation throughout the contest period.

7. Prizes and Distribution

Prize pools are composed of contest entry fees. Prizes are distributed among the top 8 ranked participants based on predefined percentages.

Cash prizes are paid exclusively via PayPal. Winners must provide a valid PayPal email address.

8. Prize Claiming

Winners will be notified through the App. Payout details must be provided within 7 days of notification.

Failure to claim a prize within this period may result in forfeiture.

9. Taxes

Winners are solely responsible for any applicable taxes, fees, or reporting obligations.

10. Disqualification

We reserve the right to disqualify participants for violations of these rules, suspected fraud, or abusive behavior.

11. Disclaimer

This contest is not sponsored, endorsed, administered by, or associated with Apple Inc.

12. Changes to Rules

We may update these Contest Rules at any time. Continued participation constitutes acceptance of the updated rules.
